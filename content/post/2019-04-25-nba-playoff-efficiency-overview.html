Welcome, below is a graph putting 2018-19 teams’ regular season SODD (offensive efficiency minus defensive efficiency), and plots them against historical belligerents. This isn’t meant to be a sort of end-all-be-all analysis, but it does serve as an interesting insight to which team might be able to win the NBA title. For example, I wouldn’t get hopes up over a team with a SODD below 5. In fact, a team hasn’t won the NBA title with SODD below the mean line since the 2015-16 Cleveland Cavaliers led by LeBron James.

That being said, as of right now we’re left with four true contenders in respect to SODD: - Milwaukee Bucks - Golden State Warriors - Toronto Raptors - Boston Celtics
