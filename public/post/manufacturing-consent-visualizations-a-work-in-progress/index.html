Manufacturing Consent… Visualizations. A Work in Progress

28 Jun 2019, 00:00

In times like these! On one hand you might be saying to yourself, “oh great another white male found Chomsky.”

But I say, what’s the data behind Manufacturing Consent. Besides the anti-neoliberal purview of the book, both Noam Chomsky and Edward S. Herman took care in sourcing a carefully worded thesis fueled by data.

I can’t quite remember where I’d heard about how Chomsky and Herman composed the book. Apparently Herman shouldered the bulk of the writing while also tabulating the data via typewriter. Herman would mail what he’d written to Chomsky and the two would continue to collaborate over post.

Talk about old, man.

Anyways, I encourage those reading this to watch this linked article which has Noam Chomsky talk about his book thirty years after its publication.

Now I’m not going to get into the book here, and try to leave the tables and vizzes as presented at the time of publication. If you want explanations then read the book. Check it out from your local library, please.
